About Me

A student of every tradition.

Brett’s spiritual journey has been an exploration of many different schools of thought, seeking the universal truths that lie at the heart of all religions and philosophies. With an open mind and a deep curiosity, he has delved into the teachings of Eastern mystics, Western philosophers, and indigenous wisdom keepers — always searching for new insights into the nature of existence.

Through his studies, he discovered that while each tradition has its own approach and language, at their core they all point to the same fundamental truth.

The power of the breath.
